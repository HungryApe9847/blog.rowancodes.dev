# I want to create a MUD.
(otherwise known as a Multi-User Dungeon.) It's basically an early form of online gaming,
where everything is text and ASCII art instead of visuals. More like D&D, but in text. Players kinda read descriptions of 
rooms and then can type in stuff like *"Go North"*, *"Kill Goblin"* or *"Take Sword"*.

I play one often, called Aardwolf (see <a href = "https://www.aardwolf.com/">here</a>) which is pretty cool, so I understand a good
gist of what they are like. So yeah, I'll say more soon!
